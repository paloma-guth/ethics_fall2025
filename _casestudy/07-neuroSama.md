My experience watching her

For AI-driven VTubers, the viewer experience is often framed as the "human’s struggle against the AI’s chaos", which "becomes the main performance". You can argue that this "chaos" (erratic, vulnerable behavior) acts as a modern "cute cue," making the VI entertaining and non-threatening, thus sustaining engagement.
